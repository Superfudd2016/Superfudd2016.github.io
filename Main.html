Main Mode

The story follows Kirby, King Dedede, Meta Knight, and Bandana Waddle Dee trying to help Magolor, after his ship the Lor Starcutter crashed onto Planet Popstar, Kirby's home planet. Kirby and friends have to fight various opponents to get the Lor's ship parts including the oars, both the wings, the helm, and the mast.

After the Lor Starcutter is restored, Magolor takes Kirby and the others to his home planet, Halcandra. When they enter Halcandra, the 4-headed dragon Landia, shoots the Lor Starcutter down. Magolor then asks Kirby and his friends to go stop Landia, which they do. After they slay Landia, the legendary artifact it was guarding falls of its head, causing it to spilt into four smaller dragons.

Magolor then shows up and reveals that he was planning to steal the Master Crown the whole time. He tells them that after he tried to take Landia on by himself and failed, he flees to Planet Popstar to go ask Kirby to help. Magolor had learned about Kirby from a jester named Marx. Magolor then covinced Kirby to help him fix his ship and do his job for him.

Magolor had the intention to use the power of the Master Crown to take over the universe. Kirby then followed Magolor into space and then Kirby and his firends had to fight the Lor Starcutter, the ship that they just spent all this time fixing. Afterwards they confront magolor and fight him, defeating him and free him from the Master Crown's control.



Extra Mode

Extra mode is a mode in Kirby's Return to Dreamland Deluxe where you play through main mode again with less healing items, harder bosses, and less health.

The differences include:

  • Bosses have more health and have a different color palette
  • There are less healing items in the levels
  • Your character have half the health they had in Main Mode
