Missions

There are a total of 10 different sub games in this theme park that Magolor created.

The sub games include 8 returning sub games from the Kirby franchise, these are:

  • Egg Catcher (Kirby's Adventure)
  • Samurai Kirby (Kirby Super Star)
  • Checkerboard Chase (Kirby and the Crystal Shards)
  • Bomb Rally (Kirby: Nightmare in Dreamland)
  • Crackity Hack (Kirby and the Amazing)
  • Smash Ride (Kirby Squeak Squad)
  • Kirby on the Draw (Kirby Super Star Ultra)
  • Ninja Dojo (Kirby's Return to Dreamland)

    • There are also two brand sub games new to the franchise in this game, these are:

  • Magolor's Tome Trackers (Kirby's Return to Dreamland Deluxe)
  • Booming Blasters (Kirby's Return to Dreamland Deluxe)

    • There are a total of 100 missions required to earn 100% on the save file, two of which require you to have a Nintendo Switch Online Membership. These two missions are to do with Samurai Kirby Online.

    After you complete the first 100 missions Magolor gives you, yuo will then recieve 20 more missions named "True Extra Missions". After you complete these 20 more missions, you receive track "Deluxe Master!" a track you would originally receive after earning 100% on Kirby's Return to Dreamland for thwe Wii.
